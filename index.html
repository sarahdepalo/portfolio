Mouse Guide

Our team of three was inspired to make Mouse Guide after browsing several Disney World planning sites and realizing how overwhelming or confusing they were. Mouse Guide is our solution to making Disney trip planning easy. Once users have created an account, they can pick from four different parks and add activities, lodging, and dining from that corresponding park and add it to their itinerary. Users can also easily delete selections from their itinerary. I primarily worked on creating the data structure and setting up the MVC for the itinerary and selection pages.

Deto No Yoru - Date Night

Tired of arguing with your parnter or friends about what to watch or eat? Deto No Yoru is our group's solution to ending those painful nights of endless scrolling. Deto No Yoru generates a random anime and local restaurant, so you can easily plan your perfect date night. As part of this three person team, I was responsible for fetching information from APIs and the backend JavaScript. I also did the CSS and JavaScript for the 'Trending' page. A major focus for our group was to ensure that this web app was completely mobile responsive.

Python Pokémon

Python Pokémon is a terminal-based Pokémon RPG game. Players can choose their starter Pokémon, battle wild Pokémon, visit Nurse Joy, and even buy items from the Poké Mart to use in battle. One of the main highlights of the game is being able to choose different attacks and that Pokémon are stronger or weaker depending on their 'type' and the opponent's 'type'. For example, a water Pokémon will be stronger against a fire Pokémon. As part of this four person team, I was primarily responsible for creating the layout of the game, the starter Pokemon selection, and the Nurse Joy function. This game can be played on any terminal by following the instructions in the GitHub repository.
