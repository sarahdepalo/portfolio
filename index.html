Mouse Guide

Our team of three was inspired to make Mouse Guide after browsing several Disney World planning sites and realizing how overwhelming or confusing they were. Mouse Guide is our solution to making Disney trip planning easy. Once users have created an account, they can pick from four different parks and add activities, lodging, and dining from that corresponding park and add it to their itinerary. Users can also easily delete selections from their itinerary. I primarily worked on creating the data structure and setting up the MVC for the itinerary and selection pages.
